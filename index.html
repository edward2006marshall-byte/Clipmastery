Captions Exist to Strengthen the Hook

The caption on your clip isn't a summary. It isn't a description. Its only job is to add to the hook of the video. Every word in your caption should be working to make the viewer think: "I need to watch this."

Think of the caption as a promise. You're telling the viewer that something worth their time is about to happen in this clip — and the content needs to deliver on that promise. A great caption creates curiosity, urgency, or emotional investment in the first fraction of a second.

The Caption Formula

There's a proven structure that works consistently across platforms. Once you internalise it, writing captions becomes second nature.

The Caption Blueprint
Streamer Name → Emotion → Reason the Viewer Cares
🔒 Unlock to Reveal

The streamer name hooks fans of that creator. The emotion tells the viewer the intensity of what they're about to see. The reason they care creates the curiosity gap — the thing that makes them need to watch to find out what happened.
